    Product Overview

    Sodium Metasilicate, Anhydrous, Laboratory Grade

    Molecular Formula: Na2SiO3

    CAS Number: 6834-92-0

    Molecular Weight: 122.06

    Sodium metasilicate is used in preparation of NaZnSiO3OH, which is a novel chiral framework material that has potential application in ion exchange, adsorption and catalysis. Laboratory grade products supplied by ScienceLab are indicative of a grade suitable for general industrial use or research purposes and typically are not suitable for human consumption or therapeutic use.
